Teams building on AI platforms often lack visibility into performance degradation — and every major provider has had significant outages, quality regressions, or unexplained model drift in the past year. Stanford research documented GPT-4 accuracy dropping from 97% to 2% on identical tasks. You can't optimize what you can't see.

Six practices that help: token budgeting, model tiering, prompt versioning, evaluation pipelines, sunset planning, and token-level observability per workflow.
